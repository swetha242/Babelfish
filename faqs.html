1)What is babelfish?

Babelfish is one of the largest online learning platforms for Spanish. Babelfish's adaptive experience has gamified elements to make the learning of Spanish more fun and effective. 100 million students are learning a language with Babelfish.

2)How much does Babelfish cost?

Learning Spanish with Babelfish is absolutely free of cost. However, if you want to expand your learning beyond the first level to higher levels, we'd suggest you have a look at our payment packages-and avail a lot of discounts too!

3)Is my information safe?

When you create a Babelfish account, you choose a username and provclasse us with an email address, which is not public. The username/name you chose can be seen by other users of Babelfish. We do not sell or share personal account information with third parties.

4)Why choose Babelfish?

In Babelfish, we guarantee that all lessons are tailored specifically to meet the requirements of the Spanish language. The way an English speaker learns Spanish, for example, is different than the way an Italian speaker learns it. This way our customers benefit from useful content with a maximum learning effect.

5)What is the teaching method?

Babelfish is a self-study platform and there are no teachers, instead you learn from our self-study exercises and from other members of the community. Each course contains sets of lessons designed around a particular topic (for example: introducing yourself). The focus of each exercise within a lesson is to give you something new (usually a word or a phrase) that you can use immediately in writing or conversation.You are updated about your progress at each level.

6)How do I use Babelfish?

A lesson consists of three levels-one, exercise, which is basically a set of flashcards containing important phrases or sentences pertaining to that lesson(e.g about me). The second, a quiz, which tests how much you have learnt. And, finally, after all lessons, there is also a final quiz with mixed varieties of questions to track your progress.

7)How do I login?

Click the "Log in" button. Enter your email and password combination. You'll be redirected to your dashboard where you'll be able to see your progress and continue learning where you left off. If you are a new user, please sign up, and enter all required details.
